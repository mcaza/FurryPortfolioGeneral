Snoozelings Virtual Pet Game


Snoozelings is a browser-based cozy gamewhere you collect little plush-like creatures called Snoozelings. These friendly creatures seek companionship where they can be loved and adored.

With thousands of color combinations, a variety of different special features, dozens of clothing options, and special event applicators, there's a snoozeling for every player.

(And a player for every snoozeling!!)

Here at Snoozelings, we think taking care of our health is an important facet of our lives that can be difficult - both mentally and physically. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to optimizing our game to improve your health through a kind, welcoming community and a multitude of fun to play self care features:

  • ♡ Random Daily Affirmations
  • ♡ Hydration Reminders
  • ♡ Journal Keeping
  • ♡ No Fear of Missing Out
  • ♡ No Predatory Sales Tactics

Meet the Artist

Slothie Studios is a fully furry operated small business. I created my first fursona over 15 years ago and have been attending conventions since Condition: Zero Hour in London, Ontario. Not surprisenly, I'm a sloth!

All of my art skills are fully taught. This includes learning to make plushies by hand, coding my own virtual pet website, and any other skills needed.

I also produce enamel pins from other fandoms to celebrate my favorite media with other fans.
